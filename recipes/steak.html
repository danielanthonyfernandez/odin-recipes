Steak

Description

This juicy steak is prepared by frying onions until golden on a non-stick pan. The steak is cooked in butter for 5 mintutes on each side on high heat for a medium steak.

Ingredients

  • Uncooked Ribeye steak
  • Onions
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Fry onions until golden
  2. Sear steak and baste in butter
  3. Sear for approximately 5 minutes on each side until cooked medium
