Pizza

Description

This pizza is prepared in an oven/air fryer using pre-made flatbread, cheese, sauce, and any toppings as desired

Ingredients

  • Flatbread
  • Tomato & Basil Sauce/li>
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Spoon a thin layer of sauce onto the flat bread
  2. Add cheese and other toppings as desired
  3. Air fry for 5-6 minutes or until the flatbread is lightly golden at 375(F)/li>
